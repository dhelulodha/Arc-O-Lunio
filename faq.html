Frequently Asked Questions

Is this fest only for Architecture Colleges/Students?

No. Arch-O-Lunio is a fest open to Students from all Colleges/Schools and all courses. Come one, Come All.

What is the theme for this year?

This year’s theme is science and Technology for Sustainable Development of the state of Rajasthan.

What’s new in this edition ?

On the occasion of its 5th Anniversary, Arch-O-Lunio is being collaborated with one of the most reputed annual event of the state “Rajasthan Science Congress”.

How do I register for Arch-O-Lunio Events and Competitions?

Go to the *Registration Tab* , select the event/competition and you’re good to go!

What type of accommodation will be provided?

Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well secured separate hostels on the campus of the university.

What all should I bring along?

Nothing. Books, cosmetics, pointed objects like scissors, knives etc are not allowed. Make sure you bring your friends along, though!

When will be the registrations for On-Spot Events?

As the name suggests, On-the-Spot!

How do I reach to Arch-O-Lunio 2017?

The buses will be arranged at prime locations in Jaipur in the morning and while returning .

What are timings of the fest?

The fest will inaugurate at 11am and will last upto 9pm on 14th October, 2017.

Are there ATMs available inside?

Yes, ATM is available right at the front gate of the university.
