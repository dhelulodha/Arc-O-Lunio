About Us

Learning today…leading tomorrow

With the rapid growth of industrialization and civilization, the emanating production of buildings, comprehensively explores ways to use design and technology that contribute to more human and environmentally responsible built world.

To display the drastic adaptation in the field of design technology, World Architecture Day, started in 2005, by the Union International des Architects (UIA), is a day to show appreciation for the work architects do and to celebrate some of the great global architectural masterpieces.

In the honor of the same, an event called ARCH-O-LUNIO, hosted by Amity University, Rajasthan. Arch-O-Lunio, the name itself, demonstrates the illumination required in innovation to transform into acknowledgement of notion. Various professionals and students from all around the nation come together at one place to enhance and share the experience of designing.

Thus the Amity School of Architecture and Planning one again invites you to express your warm approval and reinforce the event, bringing forth the intention of togetherness.

Arch-O-Lunio 2017



"The first step to controlling your world is to control your culture. To model and demonstrate the kind of world you demand to live in. To write the books. Make the music. Shoot the films. Paint the art.” - Chuck Palahniuk

Arch-O-Lunio 2017 hosted by Amity School of Architecture And Planning is a manifestation of Palahniuk’s expression. This year we pay a tribute to the enriching culture of the state of Rajasthan which is a blend of history and tradition mixed with the present contemporary lifestyle. In collaboration with “Rajasthan Science Congress (RSC) 2017”, Arch-O-Lunio celebrates its 5th year anniversary with greater zeal and enthusiasm.

The event unfolds several conferences, seminars, workshops etc. that will attract students and professionals from all fields be it science or art. Aspiring architects are invited to come and have a look at the awaiting five year course experience summarized in the form of an exhibition where all the pursuing students display their works and creativity.

Innumerable On-Spot activities and Competitions will excite and encourage students from the participating colleges to present themselves in front of the crowd and represent their college at a larger than life event being held at Amity University Rajasthan.

"Khamma Ghani" to those who want to be a part of this fest. The land of Maharaja's awaits for your arrival to give you all a royal welcome.

RAJASTHAN SCIENCE CONGRESS



The principle objective of establishing Rajasthan Science Congress is to promote scientific temper and create scientific, technological and innovation capacity among the masses with a view to promote science for all and generate excellence for sustainable development in the state of Rajasthan. It is formed as voluntary/nongovernment organization of scientists, educators and teachers (all categories) for promotion, research and development of all forms of science, technology and innovation. Rajasthan Science Congress also devote its activities in the field of education, environment and economic, social and cultural and human resource development. Rajasthan Science Congress promote scientific temper and scientific studies in various area of public interest including disaster management, resource planning people's participation, democracy, good governance, education &  raining in various programmes meant for welfare or people etc. It also works for promotion of ICT in all walks of life of the people as well as spreading science literacy.

To start with the mission as the social concern of Vaigyanik Drishtikon, it has been established under auspices of Vaigyanik Drishtikon Society (VDS), which have been in existence for last decade. The society has recognition by National Council of Science and Technology Communication (NCSTC), DST, Government of India. The scientific fraternity of Rajasthan is very keen to inculcate and promote this mission of Rajasthan Science Congress. Four sessions of Rajasthan Science Congress have been completed successfully. Rajasthan Science Congress was first held in 2013 at Tagore International School, Jaipur. Rajasthan Science Congress 2014, 2015 and 2016, were held in Dr. KN Modi University, Newai, Manipal University Jaipur and JKLU Jaipur respectively. The responsibility to organize Fifth Rajasthan Science Congress is given to Amity University Rajasthan, Jaipur.
